About FreelanceCalc

Simple, free tools built for freelancers who need quick answers—no accounts, no complexity.

What is FreelanceCalc?

FreelanceCalc is a collection of simple, free tools designed to help freelancers estimate fair pricing, generate invoices, and create basic contracts. Whether you're pricing UGC content, logo design, video editing, or any other freelance service, FreelanceCalc gives you the math you need—fast.

Why FreelanceCalc?

Many freelancers struggle with pricing. You might charge too little, leaving money on the table. Or you might price yourself out of the market. FreelanceCalc removes the guesswork by letting you calculate fair rates based on your experience level, project scope, and complexity.

We believe in simplicity. No login. No tracking. No complicated software. Just open a calculator, enter your details, and get an instant price quote you can use with confidence.

What You Can Do

  • UGC Pricing: Estimate rates for TikTok-style UGC content based on experience and deliverables.
  • Logo Design: Calculate logo pricing using experience, concepts, and complexity.
  • Video Editing: Price video projects by length, complexity, and experience.
  • Invoice Generator: Create simple invoice summaries you can copy and send.
  • Contract Generator: Generate basic freelance agreements with payment terms and ownership clauses.

Philosophy

Freelancers deserve tools that are transparent, simple, and free. We built FreelanceCalc to help you price your work fairly and build confidence when dealing with clients. Every calculator is based on realistic rates and market data—no mystery math.
