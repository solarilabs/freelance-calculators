Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy.

No Personal Data Collection

FreelanceCalc does not collect, store, or process any personal information. We do not ask you for your name, email, phone number, or any other identifying data unless you choose to enter it into a calculator form (which stays only in your browser).

No User Accounts

You do not need to create an account, log in, or sign up to use FreelanceCalc. All tools are freely available to anyone who visits the site.

Local Calculations

All pricing calculations, invoices, and contracts are generated entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers. When you close the page, your information is deleted.

No Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or any tools to monitor your activity. Your browsing behavior is completely private.

Future Advertising

As the site grows, we may add Google AdSense or similar advertising services. These third-party tools may use cookies to serve relevant ads, but we will not collect or share personal information for advertising purposes.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, feel free to reach out. We believe in transparency and simplicity.
